Town Meeting Archive

Annual and Special Town Meeting warrants with article summaries, board recommendations, and full text.

This archive covers Town Meeting warrants for 2016–2026. Each page summarizes all warrant articles with one-click access to the full official text, filterable by category and sortable by dollar amount.

Each article shows the Select Board and Finance Committee recommendation as recorded in the official warrant. Most articles require a simple majority vote; zoning articles require a two-thirds vote under G.L. c. 40A §5.
